# weather-application

<a href='https://whichweatherapplication.netlify.app/'> TRY IT HERE </a>

The purpose of this application is for use of accessing weather data from locations around the world. 

Want to know the weather for your current location? Click the "My Location" button. 

Want to know what the weather is like on the other side of town or the other side of the world? Click on the "Find a Location" button. 

This weather application tells you the current temperature, the highs and lows for the day, the next 24-hour forecast, as well as the 7-day forecast. Scroll down to find wind speeds, humidity, and a dynamic graph of the high and low temperatures for the week.
